Soccer

    Items needed:

  • 1 Soccer Ball
  • 2 Soccer Goals
  • Referee
  • 24 Players

    Rules:

  1. You can only use your feet unless you're the Goalie
  2. Most of the time you have 3 people on attack, 4 on defense, and 4 midfielders with one Goalie
  3. You can pass the ball to any player on your team. The goal is to score more than your opponent
  4. If you get the ball in the soccer goal then you get a point. If you get the ball in your own goal then the other team gets a point
  5. If the ball goes out of bounds then you use your hands and throw it over your head in one fluid motion to your teammates
  6. Whoever touched the ball before it went out the other team gets the ball
  7. You can slide tackle only if you are going for the ball, never slide tackle someone from behind or it will be a foul
  8. If a player is fouled then a free kick is awarded. If the foul is inside the box then it will be a penalty kick
  9. If you're taking a penalty kick the rest of the players have to be outside the box before the ball is kicked
  10. Once the ball is kicked the rest of the players have the opportunity to get the ball
  11. If the ball goes out behind the goal then it will be a corner or a goal kick
  12. If the ball goes out on the attacking team then it will be a goal kick, if it goes out on the defending team then it will be a corner kick
